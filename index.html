Happy 4th of July!

Celebrating 250 Years of American Independence

This year marks the 250th Anniversary of the United States of America. The historic milestone is known as the Semiquincentennial.

Here are the events where you and your family can enjoy a public fireworks display on Independence Day:

  • El Paso Convention Center
  • Ascarate Park Fourth of July Celebration
  • Downtown El Paso America 250 Celebration
  • Wet N' Wild Waterworld Fireworks Extravaganza
  • Western Playland Fireworks Celebration

Event Locations & Details

Event Location Details
El Paso Convention Center 1 Civic Center Plaza, El Paso, TX Projection light show at 10 PM
Ascarate Park 6900 Delta Drive, El Paso, TX Parking available on site. Fireworks at 9 PM
Downtown El Paso San Jacinto Plaza Fireworks at 9 PM
Wet N' Wild Waterworld S Desert Blvd, Anthony, TX Fireworks at 9 PM
Western Playland 1249 Futurity Dr, Sunland Park, NM Fireworks at 9 PM

Tips for day of the event:

  1. Arrive early to find parking
  2. Carry refillable water bottles
  3. Bring foldable chairs
  4. Enjoy live music and food vendors
  5. Watch the fireworks show
